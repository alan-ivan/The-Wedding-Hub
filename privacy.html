Privacy Policy

Introduction

The Wedding Hub ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services, including our User and Admin Extensions, Library, and associated tools.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, Email address, Payment information (collected via third-party processors such as Google Workspace Marketplace or other platforms).
  • Usage Data: Data related to your interaction with our tools, including Guest management records, Task tracking inputs, Budget allocation details, Vendor coordination data, and License activation and usage logs.
  • Technical Data: IP address, Device and browser type, Error logs generated by our Library for troubleshooting.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our tools and services.
  • Process and manage licenses.
  • Send updates, reminders, and notifications.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your information. However, we may share data with:

  • Service Providers: To support our services (e.g., Google Workspace, email services, or cloud hosting platforms).
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.
  • Comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data, including secure hosting, encryption, and restricted access. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and correct your data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Restrict or object to processing of your data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@alanivan.com.

Third-Party Services

The Wedding Hub may integrate with third-party services (e.g., Google Workspace). Please review the privacy policies of these services to understand how they handle your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on our GitHub Pages site. Continued use of our services signifies acceptance of these changes.

Contact Us

For questions or concerns, contact us at info@alanivan.com.
